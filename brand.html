I created this logo because one of my true goals, once I become successful, is to establish a makeup brand that embodies simplicity yet radiates elegance. The name ‘Lunara’ comes from ‘Luna,’ since I have always loved the moon, and ‘ara,’ which means sacred place, together symbolizing my vision of a company that is not just an ordinary brand, but one that leaves a lasting mark on people, especially women. Makeup, after all, is one of the reasons we gain confidence, and I want Lunara to represent that empowerment. The image of the girl behind the letter ‘L’ reflects the sacred beauty of a woman’s face, while the colors in the logo are my favorites, especially pink, a shade that never fails to make me smile whenever I see it. This logo is not just a design; it is a reflection of my dream, my passion, and my hope to inspire confidence and elegance in every shade.
